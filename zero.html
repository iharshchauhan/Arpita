CI & UI/UX Design, Development
Zero Rock Entertainment is the startup company to distribute the online games. They bring the unique and rookie games from Korea and distribute these to a worldwide audience. Company requested me to create CI and business website.

My Roles

I conducted research the game publishing industries. Created not only CI, UI/UX design but also develop the website. I was inspired CI idea by Zero Rock company name so I chose the metal, stone texture and typography. Also, I created the single page webiste becuase there were not much contents. Therefore, all contents were on the same page with the Anchor navigation and sticky header. Sticky header should be very helpful to jump to the certain contents easily whatever users want to see.
