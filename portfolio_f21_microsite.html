Microsite

Forever 21 Plus Size Microsite
Forever 21 website has 4 main categories such as Women, Men, Plus Size, and Kids. Recently, e-commerce consumer's needs and shopping behavior are changing rapidly. Personalized shopping experience is the most notable change in e-commerce. Executive team wanted to build each microsites to more aggressive approach to each target audience.

My Role

The project was split into three phases. The first phase was to benchmark competitors that have plus size lines and personalized shopping experience functions. The second phase was to research plus size consumer’s shopping behavior as they have totally different behavior and type of consumption. The third phase was to maintain Forever 21 identity.
