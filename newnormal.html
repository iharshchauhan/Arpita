New Normal

Create Prototype
"The New Normal" is the first cannabis commercial campaign. Need to buildup the teaser website before the commercial reveal on the air. The New Normal delves into the American history of cannabis, from pre-prohibition to today's modern industry, ultimately serving to further support MedMen's vision for marijuana legalization and regulation. The piece reverts back to pivotal moments throughout American history, such as George Washington’s hemp farm and Reefer Madness propaganda, detailing coinciding injustices of the past while looking towards a hopeful future.
