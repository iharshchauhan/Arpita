Polaris

UI/UX Design & Design System
"Polaris" is Internal Business Content Management Website. It is similar to e-Commerce CMS but there are POS features. Apple TV+ cross-functional team are managing and tracking all vendors, streaming services, finance, cast & crew members, and etc through Polaris.

Problems

Apple Team and vendors were suffering from resizing symbols and creating consistent design. Therefore, designs were inconsistent and it's not easy to update. Also, there were no any tutorials so new members can't understand design system.

Solution

I created Vuekit and Polairs library with "Smart Layout" from scratch. "Smart Layout" is a new feature of Sketch. This feature makes Symbols much more flexible, resizable, and manageable for consistent design. Moreover, I created a video tutorial for Junior and new design members.
