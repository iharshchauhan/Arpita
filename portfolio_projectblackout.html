Project Blackout BI

SG Interactive
SG Interactive (Formal Ntreev USA) was the online game distributor in USA and Canada. They brought “Point Black” from Korea but they couldn’t distribute it with same name and logo in USA. Key stakeholders made decision to change name to “Project Blackout” with keeping original game concept.

My Role

My key responsibilities were to design Project Blackout website and new logo. Executive team already recognized my graphic skills and experiences sot they assigned me this project. I managed my design team for other projects and I immersed myself deeply in this project for the perfect output. First of all, played game to understand and grasp key points features for design. Finally, it was remarkable and successful project.
