Ez Texting
Ez Texting is part of the trusted CallFire family of companies. Stakeholders desired to revamp Ez Texing BI and the website. When I started working as Sr. UI/Visual Designer, they assigned me this project. Generally, CallFire has built websites with Bootstrap which is free framework based on responsive design.

My Role

I worked on this as Sr. UI/Visual Designer for changing BI (Business Identity). Also, I worked as UI Designer for re-designed website with Bootstrap. I collaborated with web developer and marketing director. I presented variety of BI options to employees. Finally, I surveyed opinions and finished the final BI & Website between February and March 2014.
