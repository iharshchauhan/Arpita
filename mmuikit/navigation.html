Navigation

There are 2 types of navigation such as Hamburger Menu & Tab Menu.

Hamburger Menu

Tab "Hamburger Menu" icon, menu slider comes from left to right slightly with "X" button to close menu slider.

Tab Menu

Selected menu have a orange highted on the bottom. There is no swipe and maximum are 5 menus. Whenever click the menu, contents area change smoothly.
